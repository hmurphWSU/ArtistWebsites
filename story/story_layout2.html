Musical Career

As touched on in the background section, Rodrigo's music was influenced by her parent's music choice and country music. At the time, one of those country singers she followed was Taylor Swift, who she has said has influenced her music specifically. During 2020, she signed with Interscope Records and Geffen Records, and then released her debut song drivers license in January 2021 that broke Spotify's record of most daily streams of a non-holiday song twice. After drivers license, Rodrigo released deja vu, and good 4 u, neither of which quite reached the heights of drivers license, but good 4 u did come close. After those 3 songs, Rodrigo released the Sour album in May of 2021, which topped the charts in the US, Australia, Canada, Denmark, Ireland, Netherlands, Norway, New Zealand, Sweden, and the UK. Background Info

Olivia Rodrigo was born on February 20, 2003 in Murrieta California, although she lived and grew up in a the nearby city of Temecula. She is biracial, with her parents coming from the Phillipines and having German and Irish ancestry. Although Rodrigo's parents aren't really involved in the acting or music, she has been working toward her career for most of her life. She began vocal lessons in kindergarten and learned to play piano. At the age of six, she began taking acting and singing lessons all while being influenced by her parent's music choice of alternative rock music, and her own liking of country music.

Acting Career

Early on in her career, throughout the years 2015 and 2019, Rodrigo had a few different acting roles. Chronologically, she first did an Old Navy commerical, then played the lead role of Grace Thomas in the film An American Girl: Grace Stirs up Success, and then she has played two different roles which I would say are her main roles, hence the images. The first big role comes in form of playing Paige Olvera in Bizaardvark, which, interestingly also features YouTuber and boxer Jake Paul, and finally, she plays the role of Nini in High School Musical: The Musical: The Series which is also where one of her bigger songs, All I Want, comes from.
